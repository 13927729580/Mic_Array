This application reads and processes 8 digital microphones and generate 8 channels output to USB port.
Channel 0 to 6: audio data from microphone 0th to microphone 6th (16Khz)
Channel 7: audio data after do the Superdirectivity Beamforming (16Khz)

List of ICs:
ST microcontroller Cortex M7: STM32F746IGT6
ST MEMS microphones (MP45DT02) 
Audio Signal Processing STA321MPL
CS43L22 Audio Codec Control
